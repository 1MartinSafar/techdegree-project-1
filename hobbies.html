Spanish: My current approach to learning Spanish is using Duolingo to write down (e.g. in Word) words and use a dictionary such as SpanishDict to take notes on many (or all) the meanings rather than purely limiting myself to Duolingo meanings of the words only - plus a dictionary helps you understand the use of different prepositions in-depth. I highly suggest you divide the words into categories such as: Verb, El Nouns (masculine), La Nouns (feminine) and Others (here go adjectives/adverbs/prepositions/etc.) - doing it this way makes it a lot easier not to get overwhelmed by mixing nouns with other meanings (there may be many meanings for nouns and many meanings/uses as a different type - creating a huge block of information that's very hard to go through word by word)
